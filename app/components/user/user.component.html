This app is for calculating Simple interest and Compound interest.

Notes:
  • There are no units assigned for money and time in this app.
  • For money you can just enter the amount irrespective of the currency.
  • For time you can enter the number of days or months or years i.e. number of periods(time)
  • paid interest is the amount you receive/pay for the values entered.
  • All the above are the same for both simple and compound interest.
